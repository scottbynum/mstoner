The TWU Experience

What makes attending the TWU School of Management transformational? It’s a combination of inspiring moments in the classroom, supportive faculty and staff, the close community spirit, and the School’s dedication to helping students realize their full potential in the 21st century.

We will prepare you for the changing needs of business, industry, health care, government, and education and set you up for success in some of the nation’s fastest growing industries.

Because we offer a personalized learning environment with small class sizes, you will be immersed in a vibrant community of faculty and peers who share a passion for business.

Our Academic Programs

Our programs and courses are offered in three formats: face-to-face, hybrid, or online. Face-to-face and hybrid courses are offered at a variety of day and time combinations. The format of each program varies and more specific information is available in the description of each program.

Dynamic Learning Opportunities

Cooperative Education (Co-op)

Co-op allows you to connect academic knowledge with the business and professional world. Co-op expands your education outside the classroom to paid employment in a work setting.

Benefits of Co-op:

  • Apply classroom theory to actual work situations.
  • Test your interests and develop long-range career plans and career goals.
  • Enhance your marketability with on-the-job training.
  • Strengthen your problem-solving skills, self-confidence, self-discipline, and sense of responsibility.
  • Develop management skills through interactions in career settings.
