Through an endowment, your gift is invested and pays out a yearly distribution, which goes to support an area of your preference. Many times, endowed gifts go to support the most important assets of the university, our students and faculty, by providing scholarship, fellowship and professorship support. A minimum amount is required to establish an endowment. However, an endowment can be built up with smaller gifts over a period of time.

Our Priorities

Your gifts support TWU, its people, and its programs.

TWU is a state-assisted (rather than a state-supported) institution. The primary way in which we accomplish this mission is by garnering non-government financial support from a variety of areas.

Gift support from alumni, faculty, staff, corporations, foundations, friends and supporters of TWU provide critically needed funding to literally every area of the university. The trained staff at the Office of University Advancement not only solicits these charitable donations, but also performs the gift receipting for income tax purposes and stewardship duties for donor needs.
